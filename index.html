Your sales history lies. This finds the demand underneath — then forecasts it.

A working demand-planning engine: it de-censors stockouts, strips one-off outliers without touching seasonality, segments every SKU by demand pattern, backtests a portfolio of models per series, and keeps only the ones that beat a seasonal-naive baseline (Forecast Value Added).
